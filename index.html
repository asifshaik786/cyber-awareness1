KNOW THE THREATS

Common Online Scams

Six scams responsible for most of the financial fraud affecting rural families today.

📞

OTP Fraud

OTP మోసం

Fake bank calls trick you into sharing your one-time password.
💳

UPI Fraud

UPI మోసం

Fake payment requests that actually withdraw money from your account.
💼

Fake Job Scams

నకిలీ ఉద్యోగ మోసాలు

High-paying work-from-home offers that ask for a fee upfront.
💬

WhatsApp Scams

WhatsApp మోసాలు

Messages from unknown numbers pretending to be a relative in trouble.
📲

Fake Loan Apps

నకిలీ లోన్ యాప్‌లు

Illegal apps that steal your data and harass your contacts for repayment.
📸

Social Media Fraud

సోషల్ మీడియా మోసం

Fake profiles that build trust, then ask for photos or money.

THE PATTERN

How Scammers Trick People

Almost every scam — phone, WhatsApp, or social media — follows the exact same four steps.

1
📩

Message or Call

An unexpected call, SMS, or message arrives — often urgent-sounding.

→
2
🤝

Gain Trust

They claim to be your bank, a relative, or an employer to sound legitimate.

→
3
🗂️

Collect Information

They ask for OTP, PIN, bank details, or a small advance payment.

→
4
⚠️

Steal Money

Money is withdrawn or transferred — usually within minutes.

💡 Breaking the chain at ANY step stops the scam. The easiest place to break it: never share OTP, PIN, or pay any advance fee.
